About the artist

I’m Sophie, a self-taught illustrator currently studying business and creating art in my free time. I love mixing atmospheric lighting with bold, saturated palettes.

My work is inspired by cinema, science-fiction and everyday moments that feel a bit surreal. I’m available for cover art, poster design and personal commissions.

Currently exploring a series of works around urban loneliness, neon lights and reflections on wet streets.
